Instructions on how to create your event:

  1. Tell us who you are
  2. Decide whether you want the even to be private or public
  3. Choose where the even will be located
  4. Describe the event so that people can know what is happening
  5. Invite co-hosts
  6. Submit and wait to get your approval email
  7. Once the email received, your event will be published for others to see
